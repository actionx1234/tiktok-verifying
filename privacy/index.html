Privacy Policy

App Name: Midnight Files
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Midnight Files handles information when the channel creator uses this App to manage and publish original content to TikTok. This Privacy Policy is separate from our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Midnight Files, we may access:

  • Basic TikTok profile information (Open ID, display name) via user.info.basic
  • An OAuth access token stored locally on your device to authenticate API requests
  • Upload logs for tracking published content

We do not collect passwords, payment details, or any personal data beyond what is strictly required for TikTok integration.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to authenticate with TikTok's API on your behalf and to publish original video content that you explicitly choose to upload to your TikTok account.

4. Data Storage

OAuth tokens and account data are stored locally on the creator's own device. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond what is required by TikTok's API.

5. Data Sharing

Midnight Files does not sell, trade, or share your information with any third parties.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke Midnight Files access from your TikTok account settings at any time.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

For questions, please visit the Midnight Files homepage.
