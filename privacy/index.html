Privacy Policy

App Name: Midnight Files
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Midnight Files collects and uses your information when you use our automated content management service. This Privacy Policy is separate from our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Midnight Files, we may collect:

  • TikTok profile information: Open ID, display name, avatar
  • OAuth access token stored locally to authenticate API requests
  • Upload records and logs

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or personal data beyond what is required for TikTok integration.

3. How We Use Your Information

Midnight Files uses collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok API on your behalf
  • Upload and publish pre-produced video content to your TikTok account
  • Maintain upload logs for scheduling and error tracking

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and account information are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers beyond what is required by TikTok API. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

Midnight Files does not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Your data is used solely for operating this App.

6. TikTok Data Practices

When using TikTok API, your data is also subject to TikTok Privacy Policy. We comply with all TikTok developer policies regarding data usage and storage.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke Midnight Files access from your TikTok account settings at any time.

8. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Midnight Files homepage.
