Midnight Files

Midnight Files is an automated content management tool that schedules and posts short-form videos to TikTok for entertainment channels.

Terms of Service

By using this application, you agree to TikTok's Terms of Service and Developer Terms. This app accesses only the permissions necessary to post video content on your behalf.

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect or store personal user data beyond what is required for TikTok API authentication. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used solely for posting video content. No data is shared with third parties.
