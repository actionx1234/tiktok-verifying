AI-Powered Mystery Content

Midnight Files is an automated content management tool that produces and schedules short-form mystery and true crime videos for entertainment channels on TikTok and YouTube.

Our system uses AI to research, script, and produce high-quality video content, which is then automatically uploaded to connected social media accounts at optimal times.

div>

About This App

The Midnight Files TikTok integration uses the Content Posting API to automatically upload pre-produced short-form entertainment videos to TikTok. The app authenticates via OAuth 2.0 and uses video.upload and video.publish scopes to post videos directly to the connected TikTok account.

Videos are produced locally and scheduled for upload at optimal engagement times. No user data is collected or shared with third parties.
